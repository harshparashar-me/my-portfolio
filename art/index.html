Common Questions

What sizes are available?
Standard size is A4 (21 x 29.7 cm). You can upgrade to a bigger A3 size (29.7 x 42 cm) for an additional ₹200. Custom sizes available on request.
Do you provide framing?
Yes! All packages have a "With Frame" option. We use high-quality, durable synthetic wood frames (Black/Brown) with glass protection.
How much is shipping?
Shipping charges are a flat ₹100 for all packages across India. We use trusted courier partners for safe delivery.
Do you need high-quality photos?
The better the photo, the better the sketch. Clear, close-up photos with good lighting work best. However, I can work with older/blurrier photos too—just send them over for a check!
What are the payment terms?
I take a 50% advance to start the work. Once the sketch is complete, I will share a preview. You pay the remaining 50% before shipping.
Can I ask for changes?
Yes, minor changes (like shading adjustments) are allowed after the preview. Major structural changes (like changing the face angle) are not possible once drawn.
